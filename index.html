FIGHT STREETER

FIGHTING LIKE NEVER BEFORE

We proudly introduce FIGHT STREETER, a brand-new and totally modernized experience to play with your friends. We have re-imagined a decennia old concept of one-to-one fighting games with revolutionary technologies and state-of-the-art programming.

For years, we've been forced to fight with bulky controllers if we didn't want to hurt ourselves and others. Now be prepared for the immersive technique of real life motion capture, which makes the character follow your movements and make him punch and kick just as hard as you do, without actually hitting someone and risking a sentence!

Artificial Intelligence has always had a hard time to create an immersive and fun gaming experience. Our advanced machine learning system will make your enemy harder to beat and it gives you a new ultimate challenge.

With the addition of our ultramodern sound-engine, we created the best gaming experience known to man, adding change in sound when the tension is almost getting too high for comfort.

Of course we know not everyone has access to motion capture. That is why we also give the player the option to use a controller or keyboard, making not only the most immersive, but also the most diverse way to play games.

THE PROGRESS OF ART

Organization. It may even be more important than the final product itself. Especially when working in a larger group. Because bad organization will result in a bad product, or no product at all.

That is why we all documented the entire process of creating this game, to make it the best product we can deliver. The documentation can be found with a download link under 'Downloads' on this website.
